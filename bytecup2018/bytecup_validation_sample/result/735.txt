Ingredients:1 cup soy sauce 1/4 cup diced green pepper 1/4 cup packed brown sugar 4 teaspoons chopped onion 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger 2 garlic cloves, minced 4 pork loin chops (1 inch thick) 2 teaspoons sugar 2 teaspoons cornstarch 1/2 cup waterDirections:In a blender, combine the soy sauce, green pepper, brown sugar, onion, ginger and garlic; cover and process until smooth

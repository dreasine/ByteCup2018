Ingredients:1 can (15 ounces) solid-pack pumpkin 2 cups sugar 1 cup canola oil 4 large eggs 2 cups all-purpose flour 2 teaspoons baking powder 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon 1 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon salt FILLING:1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened 1/3 cup sugar 1 large egg Confectioners' sugar, optionalDirections:In a large bowl, beat the pumpkin, sugar, oil and eggs until well blended

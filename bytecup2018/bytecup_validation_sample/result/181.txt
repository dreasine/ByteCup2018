Ingredients:6 ounces boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-size pieces 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms 2 tablespoons chopped onion 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 garlic clove, minced 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour 1 cup Alfredo sauce 3/4 cup 2% cottage cheese 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons shredded Parmesan cheese, divided 1 large egg, lightly beaten 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley flakes 4 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained 1-1/2 cups shredded part-skim mozzarella cheeseDirections:In a large skillet, saute the chicken, mushrooms and onion in oil until chicken is no longer pink

Ingredients:1-1/2 pounds lean ground beef (90% lean) 1 large onion, chopped 1 large green pepper, chopped 1 jalapeno pepper, minced 1-1/4 cups soft bread crumbs 1 large egg, beaten 1 tablespoon dried parsley flakes 1 teaspoon dried basil 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning 1 teaspoon salt 1/8 teaspoon pepper 2 cans (8 ounces each) tomato sauce, divided 2 medium tomatoes, coarsely chopped 4 to 5 medium zucchini 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheeseDirections:In a large bowl, combine first 11 ingredients and one can of the tomato sauce; mix well

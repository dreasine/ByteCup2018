Ingredients:1/4 cup lime juice 1 green onion, finely chopped 2 tablespoons paprika 2 tablespoons canola oil 1 tablespoon finely chopped jalapeno pepper 1-1/2 teaspoons sugar 2 garlic cloves, minced 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1 teaspoon grated lime zest 1/2 teaspoon salt 1 beef top sirloin steak (about 1-1/2 pounds)Directions:In a small bowl, combine the first 10 ingredients
